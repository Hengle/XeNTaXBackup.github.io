Mesh Finder (Android) pre alpha
It is designed to quickly get meshes with all the main attributes, 
including positions, normal vectors, texture coordinates, 
and, if necessary, to convert them to Obj (Wavefront .obj file)
analog for PC (Advanced Mesh Reaper, ModelResearcher, Hex2Obj).

[link on github](https://github.com/Durik256/MeshFinder-Android-)
