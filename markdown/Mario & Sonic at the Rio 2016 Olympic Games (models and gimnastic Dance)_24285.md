It seems that your PAC files are supported by HedgeLib [https://github.com/Radfordhound/HedgeLib](https://github.com/Radfordhound/HedgeLib)
You can download latest build here [https://ci.appveyor.com/project/Radford ... /artifacts](https://ci.appveyor.com/project/Radfordhound/hedgelib/build/job/t9d15ib2pbq0d6yf/artifacts)


And you can use it like this:

```
HedgeArcPack.exe -T=rio -E pea_anim_gymcln_BRZ.pac out_dir
```


Check also this article [http://wiki.xentax.com/index.php/Sonic_Games_PAC](http://wiki.xentax.com/index.php/Sonic_Games_PAC)
