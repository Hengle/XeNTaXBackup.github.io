Some additional info: 

> KSV stands for Kheops Studio Video and is a successor of Cryo' HNM format. Overall file structure mostly similar to HNM4/HNM6 files. Video is XviD-encoded and audio is stored in Cryo APC format. 
>
> 
>
> [edit]Games Using KSV 
>
> Echo: Secret Of The Lost Cavern 
>
> Return To Mysterious Island 
>
> The Secrets of Da Vinci: The Forbidden Manuscript 
>
> Voyage: Journey To The Center Of The Moon
